About Sam Vance

TL;DR here's my resume

My name is Sam Vance, I am 22 years old and finishing my undergraduate at BYU! I am studying Computer Science with an emphasis in Machine Learning and a minor in Mathematics. I am hoping to get into a Master's or PhD program to continue my studies, with the goal of entering industry creating RL algorithms for robotics applications. Outside of school I love to rock climb (I climb v8/9 and ~5.13a indoors), play the piano, sketch, build robots, and I am actually starting some Bonsai Trees. Cross your fingers for me. I am creating this website as a place to showcase some of the projects that I have been working on.

I have been building robotics projects for several years now, and never really had an effective way to showcase them. I tried to do TikTok for a while, and I may get back into making videos, but videos take a long time to make, and an even longer time to make them engaging. Also frankly, it is hard to have a tight enough publishing schedule to build an audience, so for now TikTok has taken a back seat. I have a lot of projects that I have built and will be adding them to this website as I have time to do so. For now I have a few of my favorites up here.

Currently the projects page just shows some photos and videos that I have taken of the projects, along with a little bit of technical information about them. In the future, I plan to add more detailed information including CAD files, code, and more detailed descriptions of the projects. Hopefully if people are interested, this website will be a good resource to get started with some robotics projects.
